cow farm

In a seemingly pleasant farm with hedges that appear to be forming a maze, you are a cow standing at the entrance. A young girl wanders with you but is intangible and randomly teleports while moving around the farm. Surrounded by these mysterious elements, what exactly is going on and where exactly should you go?


Controls:
W - go up | A - go left | S - go down | D - go right
E - eat grass
Space bar - jump if facing left or right; smile if facing front or back
*Once idle, the cow will have an infuriated expression
*Randomly, the cow gets moved by unknown forces
